Julien Martin

Managing Partner

With over 14 years of experience in corporate real estate and private equity, Julien has managed $1.8 billion assets for an American investment fund. His expertise spans strategic planning, finance, structuring complex deals and  high-stakes negotiations.

Based in Russia, he combines a global business perspective with an in-depth understanding of cultural nuances, and commercial dynamics.

Beyond his expertise, Julien brings a hands-on approach to projects, ensuring that every deal is adapted to the client’s operational reality. His ability to navigate complex environments, and deliver measurable results makes him a driving force behind AknoTrade’s commitment to building trusted and long-term commercial bridges between markets.

Charles Sbaire

Managing Partner

Charles is a seasoned entrepreneur and digital communicator, best known for his YouTube channel “Charles en Russie”, which has attracted over 44,900 subscribers and features nearly 150 videos exploring life, culture, and business opportunities in Russia.

Charles has cultivated a strong network of entrepreneurs, investors, and local decision-makers. His deep cultural understanding, ability to identify opportunities, and talent for fostering trust make him an invaluable bridge between international businesses and the Russian market.

Beyond content creation, his presence on the ground and his relationships across key sectors provide AknoTrade with privileged access to connections and market intelligence that others simply cannot reach.

Our DNA

When Doors Are Closed, We Open the Right Window

AknoTrade takes its name from the Russian word окно (okno, pronounced akno), meaning “window” a symbol of openness and connection.

Our mission is to be that strategic window into complex and often misunderstood Russian markets.

In a world of fragmented supply chains and shifting alliances, we build structured, transparent, and lasting bridges between producers, importers, and target markets. Our vision: a tailor-made, structured form of trade — grounded in local knowledge, contractual rigor, and a deep respect for cultural and operational realities.

Our values guide every mission, we build trust through clear agreements and flawless execution, work independently to serve only our clients’ interests, focus on strategies that deliver measurable results, adapt to each market’s codes and constraints, and keep openness, cultural intelligence, and human connection at the core of every deal.

Our method is clear and adaptable: identify and qualify opportunities, structure the right connections, and guide execution through every critical stage, from negotiation to delivery. The result: secure, efficient, and profitable access to the Russian markets.
